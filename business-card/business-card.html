Business Card

As the infallible Miss Manners once said, "You glance at an email. You give more attention to a real letter." People think of code as cold, so I wanted to bring to my brand the feeling of traditional luxury that comes with fountain pens, engraved stationery and hand-written letters.

I used Adobe Illustrator to create a logo based on a fountain pen nib. Using InDesign, I placed it on a splotch of color that shows the texture of paper beneath it and paired this image with fonts inspired by handwriting and old-world typewriters.
